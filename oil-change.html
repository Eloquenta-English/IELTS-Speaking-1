Oil Change

Basic guide • ~30 minutes • Basic tools required

1Warm the engine✓

Run for 5 minutes to thin oil for easier drainage.

2Drain oil✓

Remove drain plug. Torque: 35 Nm. Collect in pan.

Oil capacity: 4.2L • Drain plug: 17mm hex

3Replace filter✓

Lubricate new filter gasket with fresh oil. Hand-tighten + 3/4 turn.

Filter: Toyota 90917-03004 or equivalent • 76mm wrench

4Refill oil✓

Pour new oil. Check level with dipstick after 5 minutes.

Recommended: 5W-30 synthetic • ~4.2L

5Reset reminder✓

Clear service indicator. Log date and mileage.
